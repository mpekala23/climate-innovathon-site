Welcome To The Climate Innovathon


Are you interested in making a positive impact on the environment? Do you want to be part of a team that will develop innovative solutions to tackle climate change? Then look no further than the upcoming Climate Innovathon! This event is a unique opportunity to join a community of like-minded individuals and work together to pitch innovative solutions related to carbon dioxide removal.


During the three-day Climate Innovathon, participants will form teams or work solo to develop creative research proposals related to carbon dioxide removal, using a database of open questions and research topics as inspiration. They will have access to industry experts who will offer guidance and support, and will be encouraged to think critically and creatively to come up with unique solutions. After refining their proposals, teams will then pitch their solutions to a panel of judges, with the top four teams winning $100-$200.


When: In-person from 3-5 PM on 4/14 and 4/16, asynchronous in-between.

Where: Leverett Library Theater: 28 DeWolfe St, Cambridge, MA 02138
